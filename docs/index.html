Methodology

Sensors — FloodNet ultrasonic depth sensors measure street flooding in real time. Dot size and color show flood depth.

311 Complaints — Flood-related 311 reports from NYC Open Data, filtered to 9 flooding- or sewer-related categories that increase in response to heavy rain events. Each complaint fades out 20 minutes after it was filed.

Radar — NOAA MRMS Vertically Integrated Liquid (VIL) shows the intensity of the rain band overhead. VIL was chosen over base reflectivity because it isolates the intense core of the storm — a broader, less intense system surrounded it, which base reflectivity would not distinguish as clearly. Values below 4 kg/m² are hidden; the blue scale runs to 15+ kg/m².

Animation — Covers 12:23 PM – 11:00 PM ET on October 30, 2025. Use the speed control to adjust playback rate.
